# Interpretation

The theory of Agencity should be read as a framework for measuring how a
system actively organizes its evolution.

## What Agencity is not

- not a force
- not intelligence
- not consciousness
- not Shannon entropy
- not raw complexity

## What it is

Agencity is a measurable observable that quantifies how strongly a system
supports structured, self-maintained dynamics over the admissible evolution
space.
